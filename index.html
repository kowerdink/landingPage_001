Jason David Frank (September 4, 1973 – November 19, 2022) was an American actor and martial artist who portrayed Tommy Oliver in Mighty Morphin Power Rangers, Power Rangers Zeo, Power Rangers Turbo, and Power Rangers Dino Thunder, with his most recent reprisal of the role in the Power Rangers Super Ninja Steel episode "Dimensions in Danger". He also portrayed the feature film incarnation of the same character in Mighty Morphin Power Rangers: The Movie.
